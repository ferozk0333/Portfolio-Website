Mental Health Analytics using NLP

Problem Statement

Mental health challenges like stress, anxiety, and depression (SAD) are prevalent, especially among individuals aged 13–29, and have worsened due to post-pandemic isolation and economic uncertainty. Developing countries like India face a $1.03 trillion economic loss (2012–2030, WHO) from untreated mental health issues, highlighting an urgent need for accessible solutions.

Key Challenges:
  • Shortage of mental health professionals, primarily in rural areas.
  • Stigma deterring individuals from seeking help.
  • Existing apps focus on temporary relief, lacking root-cause analysis and actionable insights.

Proposed Solution

The solution uses NLP and Deep Learning to provide a personalized mental health assistant focused on identifying root causes and delivering actionable insights:

1. Write Your Mood: Users describe their day, and NLP analyzes sentiments (e.g., happy, sad, anxious).

2. Cognitive Corrections: Users input recurring troubling thoughts, categorized by themes (e.g., Family, Finance) and emotional impact.

Output: Reports showing mood trends, key thought categories, and actionable insights to assist therapists in diagnosis and monitoring.

Tech Stack

  • Programming Languages: Python
  • Databases: MySQL
  • Frontend: Streamlit
  • AI/ML Techniques: NLP, DistilBERT, Sentiment Analysis
