Data Visualization using PowerBI

Problem Statement

SMart, a retail outlet, lacked an integrated data visualization and analysis system to evaluate historical sales performance and forecast future trends. Without clear insights into regional performance, shipping efficiency, and category-level sales, strategic decision-making was limited, impacting growth and customer satisfaction.

Proposed Solution

Developed a Power BI dashboard leveraging two years of sales data to deliver actionable insights and enable data-driven decisions. Key features included:

KPI Analysis: Monitored orders, sales, profit, average shipping days, and return order percentage.

Interactive Filtering: Allowed zone-wise data exploration (West, East, North, South).

Time Series Analysis: Compared sales and profit on a monthly YoY basis.

Visualizations: Used donut charts, bar charts, and a map for category-wise sales and regional analysis.

Sales Forecasting: Provided a 15-day sales forecast using historical trends.

Insights and Recommendations:

  • Highlighted top-performing regions (West).
  • Identified preferred shipping modes.
  • Revealed seasonal sales spikes (September and December).
